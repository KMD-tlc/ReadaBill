Where to Find More Information

Statutes are most easily accessed online via the Texas Constitution and Statutes website, which is maintained by the Texas Legislative Council and is regularly updated and corrected.

Bills can be accessed electronically in the following ways:

  • via the online legislative resources Texas Legislative Information System (TLIS) or Capweb, and
  • via the public resource Texas Legislature Online (TLO).
Bills are distributed in electronic format, hard copy, or both formats to members of the legislature at certain stages of the legislative process.

For a comprehensive guide to drafting rules and word usage that may help you understand legislative documents, you may refer to the Texas Legislative Council Drafting Manual.

Need a hard copy of the Drafting Manual? Call TLC at (512) 463-1144.
